Community Development

Being able to participate as a community developer is a great privilege.
Charity is what I love doing, and being able to give back to the community is always a great feeling.
Since joining Love For Humanity in 2020, I have been provided that opportunity to give back out to the less privilege.
Eventhough we are still in the process of doing our official legalization documents, we hope to finish that soon so we can run our affairs on a national scale.
All the credits go to Mbella Irene, the founder of Love For Humanity and for giving me this platform to be able to give out to those who can't seem to afford.

Also, being a member of The Jason Foundation whose aim is to provide for the refugees in Anglophone Cameroon is also a great privilege.
You can checkout The Jason Foundation Website @ www.thejasonsfoundation.org
